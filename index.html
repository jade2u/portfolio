GOAL

I want to create a GeoGuesser-esque web game where players connect songs with their country/language.

My 2 motivations are to:
‚Ä¢ Make music discovery fun
‚Ä¢ Make international music more accessible

ITERATIONS

Double-Click

Click-and-drag to navigate and double-click on a country to translate.

Textbox

Navigate textbox on top of country and press enter to translate.

Gamify

Languages are color-coded using raycasting.
Press enter to translate and try and match the text at the top.

PROGRESS

So far, I have mostly worked on the map interaction. I am now working on making the game more fun and intuitive.


Areas to improve:

  1. Integrate the Spotify API to get song recommendations based on country.
  2. Focus gameplay on guessing the country instead of language.
  3. Make the map more conducive with mobile-use.
